# Doctor booking app 

## The requirements

A popular doctor booking application is an application introduced during Covid-19 to
help people to make booking online for a doctor consultation without physical contact.
This application provides other option for the patient to choose. The application offers
online video consultation, booking doctor’s appointments after choosing a specialist.

## Design the doctor booking application

Help me design a doctor booking application. 

With the above requirements, design a doctor booking application that addresses the issues identified in the current design. Consider the following:
- Define the problem space: What are the specific issues with the current design of doctor booking applications, and how can your design address these issues?
- Start with conceptual design: What are the core functionalities and features of your doctor booking application, and how do they align with user needs and goals?
- Metaphors: Are there any familiar concepts or metaphors that can be used to make the app more intuitive and user-friendly?
- Interaction Types: Suggest best interaction types (e.g., instructing, conversing, manipulating, exploring) for different features of the app to enhance user experience.
- Conversation Type: Suggest how the app can support conversational interactions (e.g., chatbots, voice assistants) to assist users in booking appointments and answering their queries.
- User needs and goals: What are the specific needs and goals of the users (patients, doctors, hospital staff) when using the app?
- User interface design: How can the app's interface be designed to be user-friendly and intuitive

Your response should include a clear and concise explanation of your design choices, supported by relevant examples and principles of interaction design.

Also give an alternative design for the app, and explain the differences between the two designs and the rationale behind each design choice. This will be used for A/B testing to evaluate which design is more effective in meeting user needs and improving usability.

Do not implement any code yet, just provide the design and rationale.
